# Privacy Policy for Multimodal AI Browser Agent

**Last Updated:** December 5, 2025

## 1. Data Collection
We do not collect, store, or sell any personal data on our servers. All data processing happens locally on your device or is sent directly to the AI providers (Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, Perplexity) based on your chosen configuration.

## 2. API Keys
Your API keys are stored securely in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). They are never shared with us, the extension developers, or any third parties other than the respective AI provider when you make a request.

## 3. Page Content & Web History
*   **Context Awareness:** When you ask the AI a question about the current page, the text content, title, and URL of that page are sent to the selected AI provider (e.g., Google, OpenAI) to generate an answer.
*   **Data Usage:** This data is used solely for the purpose of generating the AI response you requested. We do not store this data.

## 4. Third-Party Services
This extension acts as a client for third-party AI services. By using this extension, you are also subject to the privacy policies of the AI providers you choose to use:

*   **Google Gemini:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
*   **OpenAI:** [OpenAI Privacy Policy](https://openai.com/privacy)
*   **Anthropic (Claude):** [Anthropic Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/privacy)
*   **Perplexity AI:** [Perplexity Privacy Policy](https://www.perplexity.ai/privacy)

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
